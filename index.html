About Yamaha RX100

The Yamaha RX100 is a legendary two-stroke motorcycle that gained massive popularity in India during the late 1980s and early 1990s. Known for its lightweight design, quick acceleration, and distinctive exhaust note, the RX100 became a cult favorite among bike enthusiasts. It featured a 98cc engine and was renowned for its reliability and performance. Even today, it remains an iconic classic in the Indian motorcycling community.
